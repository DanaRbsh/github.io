Becoming a web developer

Biography

I was born and lived like all ordinary people. School - Lyceum - University. Postgraduate studies at an excellent university in the city of Kiev. Then a family, two children. And I ask myself is this really all that I can achieve in life?

My kids teach me how to work under pressure in short time conditions with high creativity level (Yep, our kids are like this). And here you are, in the door with a sign of "Make your choice". Time to decide, who are you.

My way of web development is still in the beginning. But I should say that the skills, wich I get thanks to my kids, help me to constantly improve myself, develop my skills and never give up.
